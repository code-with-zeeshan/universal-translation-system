The Universal Translation System's data pipeline implements a strategic approach to multilingual data processing. By focusing on English-centric pairs with selective direct routes, we achieve 80% efficiency gains while maintaining translation quality across all 20 supported languages. The pipeline ensures balanced representation, quality filtering, and intelligent augmentation to create production-ready training datasets.
